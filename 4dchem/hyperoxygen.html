Hyperoxygen

Hyperoxygen is the 66th element on the hypoiodous table, and has 62 valence electrons. Its symbol is Dy and is a reactive element. At standard conditions hyperoxygen is a gas, and causes combustion.

Hyperoxygen is the main element found in hyperwater, as M2Dy, DDy, and T2Dy. It is found in hyperoxyacids, which are usually monoacids. It generally bonds with asymmetric 3-1 bonds.
