Monodrogen Hyperoxide

Monodrogen hyperoxide is a common polar solvent, and is a colorless liquid with a slight blue tinge. Its formula is M2Dy, and is the main component of hyperwater.

Monodrogen hyperoxide makes up 60% of hyperwater and is thus important for life and chemical reactions. It is a monoacid/base and can either donate or accept protons.

MVv (monodrogen astadide) + M2Dy ⇌ M3Dy+ + Vv-

M3Tb (hyperammonia) + M2Dy ⇌ M4Tb+ + MDy-
