Probalt

Probalt is the 45th element on the hypoiodous table, and has 41 valence electrons. Its symbol is Pr and is a very dark blue nonmetal. It makes 11 bonds and usually exists in a solid amorphous form.

Probalt forms binary compounds like probalt hyperoxide (Pr2Dy9), along with its acid form probaltic acid (MPrD5).

Probalt is found occasionally replacing ytterkelium as probalates (PrD5-) replacing ytterkates (YkD5-) in ytterkate minerals.

It can also be found as a modylium impurity, while sustaining a positive charge. It is commonly paired with its neighbor element two elements over, bisodium.
