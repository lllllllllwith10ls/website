Universes Collide

Home Page

Various civilizations from all over the multiverse have found themselves with premature access to the Multiverse. From the ruins of a long lost civilization, known as the Gone, these species have inherited the responsibility that the Gone once had, with none of the experience of the Gone. They must handle this responsibly, or die trying.

The Multiverse

The Multiverse is a high-dimensional spacetime in which all known universes are embedded in. These spacetimes range from pristine infinite universes to shattered and shredded manifolds. The below links are some of the important universes of the Multiverse.

The Universe - A flat spacetime, home of the Humans.

Technology

Technology has been invented and harvested independently by all civilizations. These go into various technologies by the Gone or of the various species of the Multiverse.

Analogizers - Analogizers are devices created by the Gone built to make analogs of various objects, ranging from living conscious beings to random rocks found on planets. They may not be perfect, however, and repeatedly analogizing is not a good idea lest you develop conditions like glucose intolerance.
