Category 6: Hexagonal Rectates

This deals with honeycombs with hexagonal symmetric verfs. These are the "knockoff ridditdih regiments", which itself which is a "knockoff rissidtixhi regiment". This is therefore a "knockoff knockoff rissidtixhi regiment". There are several honeycombs here in 4 regiments, with one regiment with 49 honeycombs, a fissary, and 3 compounds, and the rest have 9 honeycombs, a fissary, and a compound.

Rath regiment

This regiment has thonnic symmetry.

The main four

These correspond to the main nine of the rissidtixhi regiment.

147. Rath - (RATH) rectified thon. It has trats and octs.

148. Ridthah - (RID thah) rectified dithah. It has ditathas, tazts, and octs.

149. Ridditha - (RID dith ah) retroditrigonary hexat-azat. It has ditathas and srotazts.

150. Dittitda - (Dit TIT dah) ditrigonary tet-disazat. It has octs, srotazts, and tazts.

The nine trapezics

These correspond to the sixteen trapezics of the rissidtixhi regiment.

151. Sidtha - (SID thah) small ditrigonary hexat-azat. It has trats and srotazts.

152. Sidtitta - (sid TIT ah) small ditrigonary tet-azat. It has octs and srotazts.

153. Gidtha - (GID thah) great ditrigonary hexat-azat. It has trats and tazazts.

154. Dithida - (DITH id ah) ditrigonary hexat-disazat. It has ditathas, tazts (oriented other way), and tazazts.

155. Gidtitta - (gid TIT ah) ditrigonary hexat-disazat. It has octs and tazazts.

156. Dittitita - (DIT it tit ah) ditrigonary tet-trisazat. It has octs, srotazts, tazts, and tazazts.

157. Dititada - (dit IT ad ah) ditrigonary tet-azat-disazat. It has octs, tazts, tazts (oriented other way), and tazazts.

158. Dittita - (dit IT ah) ditrigonary trisazat. It has srotazts, tazts, and tazazts.

159. Dittada - (dit AD ah) ditrigonary azat-disazat. It has srotazts, tazts (other way), and tazazts.

The holey crown

This is the holey crown of the rath regiment, and is also noble.

160. Todta - (TOD tah) toroidoditrigonary azat. It has razts.

Wild nonorientables

These are wild and have razts.

161. Ditaha - (dit AH ha) ditrigonary azat-hexat-azat. It has trats, tazts (other way), and razts.

162. Dithadda - (dith AD ah) ditrigonary hexat-disazat. It has trats, srotazts, and razts.

163. Ditatta - (dit TAT ah) ditrigonary azat-tet-azat. It has octs, tazts (other way), and razts.

164. Dititidda - (dit it TID ah) ditrigonary tet-disazat. It has octs, srotazts, and razts.

Wild orientables

These are wild and don't have razts.

165. Dithtidda - (dith TID ah) ditrigonary hexat-tet-disazat. It has trats, tazts, octs, and srotazts.

166. Dithitta - (dith IT ah) ditrigonary hexat-tet-azat. It has ditathas, octs, and srotazts.

Tritrapezics

These have razts, tazts (other way), and tazazts.

167. Ditattah - (dit TAT ah) ditrigonary trisazat-hexat. It has trats, razts, tazts (other way), and tazazts.

168. Dittiteta - (DIT ih TET ah) ditrigonary tet-tetrakisazat. It has octs, tazts, razts, tazts (other way), and tazazts.

Windowed four

These are analogous to the windowed twelve of rissidtixhi.

169. Dithititta - (DITH it TIT ah) ditrigonary hexat-tet-trisazat. It has trats, tazts, octs, tazazts, and razts.

170. Ditathatta - (dit tath AT ah) ditrigonary tet-hexat-trisazat. It has ditathas, tazts, octs, tazts (other way), and razts.

171. Dithatatta - (dith at TAT ah) ditrigonary hexat-azat-tet-azat. It has ditathas, octs, tazazts, and razts.

172. Ditadittah - (dit TAD dit ah) ditrigonary tet-disazat-hexat. It has ditathas, octs, tazts (other way), and razts.

Tha crown

This is an analog of the oho crowns.

173. Toditha - (TO dith ah) toroidoditrigonary hexat-azat. It has thas and razts.

Puckered two

These are analogs of the puckered three.

174. Ditdiha - (DIT dih hah) ditrigonary dishexat-azat. It has trats, thas, and tazts (other way).

175. Gidtidha - (GID tid hah) great ditrigonary dishexat-azat. It has ditathas, thas, and srotazts.

Tha bunch

These are analogs of the oho bunch, with thas instead of ohoes.

176. Sidtidhidda - (SID tid hid dah) small ditrigonary dishexat-disazat. It has trats, srotazts, tazazts, and thas.

177. Gidtidhidda - (GID tid hid dah) great ditrigonary dishexat-disazat. It has ditathas, tazts (other way), tazazts, and thas.

178. Dittidhidda - (DIT tid hid dah) ditrigonary dishexat-disazat. It has ditathas, tazts (other way), srotazts, and thas.

179. Dithatta - (dith THAT ah) ditrigonary hexat-trisazat. It has tazts (other way), tazazts, srotazts, and thas.

Two omnics

These are analogs of the six omnics.

180. Ditpha - (DIT fa) ditrigonary prismatohazat. It has hips and thazats.

181. Todtiph - (TOD tiff) toroidoditrigonary prismatohexat. It has hips and thats.

Holey fexahoha

This has compounds of three hexats (fexat) and hohas as cells.

182. Dithiha - (DITH ih hah) ditrigonary hexat-hexat-azat. It has hexats, hohas, and razts.

Wild fexahohas

These also have compounds of three hexats (fexat) and hohas as cells, and are additionally wild.

183. Sidthihatta - (SID thih HAT ah) small ditrigonary hexat-hexat-tet-azat. It has hexats, octs, hohas, and tazts (other way).

184. Sidthah tidda - (SID thah TID ah) small ditrigonary hexat-hexat-tet-disazat. It has hexats, octs, hohas, tazts, and tazts (other way).

185. Gidthihatta - (GID thih HAT ah) great ditrigonary hexat-hexat-tet-azat. It has hexats, octs, hohas, and srotazts.

186. Gidthah Tidda - (GID thah TID ah) great ditrigonary hexat-hexat-tet-disazat. It has hexats, octs, hohas, tazts, and srotazts.

187. Sidthah Titta - (SID thah TIT ah) small ditrigonary hexat-hexat-tet-trisazat. It has hexats, tazts, octs, hohas, tazazts, and srotazts.

188. Gidthah Titta - (GID thah TIT ah) great ditrigonary hexat-hexat-tet-trisazat. It has hexats, tazts, octs, hohas, tazts (other way), and srotazts.

Direct omnihohas

These have hohas and hips.

189. Sidtidhippa - (SID tid HIP ah) small ditrigonary dishexat-primsatoazat. It has trats, tazts (other way), hips, and hohas.

190. Sidtithappa - (SID tid HIP ah) small ditrigonary tet-hexat-primsatoazat. It has octs, tazts (other way), hips, and hohas.

Offset omnihohas

These also have hohas and hips.

191. Gidtidhippa - (GID tid HIP ah) great ditrigonary dishexat-primsatoazat. It has ditathas, tazazts, hips, and hohas.

192. Gidtithappa - (GID tid HIP ah) great ditrigonary tet-hexat-primsatoazat. It has octs, tazazts, hips, and hohas.

193. Dittitdihappa - (DIT it dih HAP ah) ditrigonary tet-dishexat-primsatoazat. It has ditathas, octs, srotazts, hips, and hohas.

194. Sidtitdhipda - (SID tit dih HIP dah) small ditrigonary tet-dishexat-primsatodisazat. It has trats, octs, tazts, srotazts, hips, and hohas.

195. Gidtitdhipda - (GID tit dih HIP dah) great ditrigonary tet-dishexat-primsatodisazat. It has ditathas, octs, tazts, tazts (other way), hips, and hohas.

Compounds

These are compounds of the ritrah or spiddihexah regiment.

F. Dittiph - (DIT iff) ditrigonary prismatohexat. It has hips and hexats.

C. Rehoh - (REE hoh) rectified hexatary hexat. It has thats and hexats.

C. Firhoh - (FIR hoh) facetorectified hexatary hexat. It has hexats and thazats.

C. Hehaz - (heh HAZ) hexat-hazat. It has thats and thazats.

Rihach regiment

This regiment has hachonnic symmetry.

The main four

196. Rihach - (ri HACH) rectified hachon. It has trats and coes.

197. Riddichassa - (rid dit CHASS ah) rectified ditchissah. It has ditathas, tezts, and coes.

198. Ridthassa - (rid THASS ah) retroditrigonary hexat-squazat. It has ditathas and srotezts.

199. Ditticdissa - (dit ic DISS ah) ditrigonary cube-dissquazat. It has coes, srotezts, and tezts.

The two trapezics

200. Dithessa - (di THESS ah) ditrigonary hexat-squazat. It has trats and srotezts.

201. Ditcissa - (dit SISS ah) ditrigonary cube-squazat. It has coes and srotezts.

The wild orientables

202. Dithcidissa - (dith si DISS ah) ditrigonary hexat-cube-dissquazat. It has trats, tezts, coes, and srotezts.

203. Dithicassa - (dith ih KASS ah) ditrigonary hexat-cube-squazat. It has ditathas, coes, and srotezts.

One omnic

204. Todithish - (to DITH ish) toroidoditrigonary hisquat-hexat. It has hexats and tehats.

Compounds

C. Dithah - (DITH ah) ditrigonary hexat-hexat. It has two types of hexats.

C. Rehash - (RE hash) rectified hexatary shexat. It has tehats and hexats.

Rihed regiment

This regiment has hedhonnic symmetry.

The main four

205. Rihed - (ri HED) rectified hedhon. It has trats and ids.

206. Riddit Dohpa - (rid dit DOE pah) rectified ditdohpa. It has ditathas, pazts, and ids.

207. Ridthappa - (rid THAP ah) retroditrigonary hexat-pazat. It has ditathas and sropazts.

208. Dittid Dippa - (DIT id DIT pah) ditrigonary doe-dispazat. It has ids, sropazts, and pazts.

The two trapezics

209. Ditheppa - (DITH ep ah) ditrigonary hexat-pazat. It has trats and sropazts.

210. Ditdippa - (dit DIP ah) ditrigonary doe-pazat. It has ids and sropazts.

The wild orientables

211. Dithid Dippa - (dith id DIP ah) ditrigonary hexat-doe-dispazat. It has trats, pazts, ids, and sropazts.

212. Dithiddipa - (dith idd IP ah) ditrigonary hexat-doe-pazat. It has ditathas, ids, and sropazts.

One omnic

213. Todithiph - (to DITH iff) toroidoditrigonary hipat-hexat. It has hexats and phats.

Compounds

C. Dithedh - (DITH ed) ditrigonary hidecat-hexat. It has hexats and thidecats.

C. Rehohap - (RE hoh hap) rectified hexatary hipat. It has phats and hexats.

Rihihexah regiment

This regiment has hihexahic symmetry.

The main four

214. Rihihexah - (RI hi hex ah) rectified hedhon. It has trats and thats.

215. Ridthihiha - (rid THI hi ha) rectified dithehihah. It has ditathas, hazts, and thats.

216. Ridthaha - (rid THAH hah) retroditrigonary hexat-hazat. It has ditathas and srohazts.

217. Dithaddiha - (DIT id DIT pah) ditrigonary doe-dispazat. It has ids, sropazts, and pazts.

The two trapezics

218. Sidtheha - (sid THEH ha) small ditrigonary hexat-hazat. It has trats and srohazts.

219. Gidtheha - (gid THEH ha) great ditrigonary hexat-hazat. It has thats and srohazts.

The wild orientables

220. Dith Hidha - (dith HID hah) ditrigonary hexat-hexat-dishazat. It has trats, hazts, thats, and srohazts.

221. Dithahiha - (dith AH ih hah) ditrigonary hexat-hexat-hazat. It has ditathas, thats, and srohazts.

One omnic

222. Todithihath - (to DITH hih hath) toroidoditrigonary hihexat-hexat. It has hexats and shexats.

Compounds

C. Dithazah - (dith AZ ah) ditrigonary hazat-hexat. It has hexats and thazats.

C. Rehohah - (RE hoh hah) rectified hexatary hihexat. It has shexats and hexats.
