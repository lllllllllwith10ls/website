Category 2: Truncates

There are 56 truncates (4 compact, 10 paracompact, 3 noncompact, 3 ditrigonaries, 8 freeloaders, 16 quasitruncates, and 12 alter-truncates). All are tame, and the compacts, paracompacts, the ditrigonaries, and the noncompacts are all orientable.

Compact truncates

30. Tih - (TIH) truncated ikhon. It has tis and does. Its verf is a trigonal pyramid.

31. Tiddoh - (TID oh) truncated doehon. It has tids and octs. Its verf is a square pyramid.

32. Tipech - (TI pech) truncated pechon. It has tics and ikes. Its verf is a pentagonal pyramid.

33. Tipped - (TIP ed) truncated pedhon. It has tids and ikes. Its verf is also a pentagonal pyramid. Tip: This is actually not pronouced how you think it would be.

Paracompact truncates

34. Thexah - (THEX ah) truncated hexah. It has toxats and tets. Its verf is a trigonal pyramid.

35. Tath - (TATH) truncated thon. It has tuts and trats. Its verf is a hexagonal pyramid.

36. Tishexah - (TI shex ah) truncated shexah. It has toxats and octs. Its verf is a square pyramid.

37. Thach - (Thach) truncated hachon. It has tics and trats. Its verf is another hexagonal pyramid.

38. Tiphexah - (TI fex ah) truncated phexah. It has toxats and ikes. Its verf is a pentagonal pyramid.

39. Thed - (THED) truncated hedhon. It has tids and trats. Its verf is yet another hexagonal pyramid.

40. Thihexah - (THI hex ah) truncated hihexah. It has toxats and trats. There seems to be an abundance of hexagonal pyramid verfs here. Thihexah is not a exception.

41. Tosquah - (TO squa) truncated squah. It has tosquats and cubes. Its verf is a trigonal pyramid.

42. Tocth - (TOCTH) truncated octh. It has toes and squats. Its verf is a square pyramid.

43. Tissish - (TISS ISH) truncated sisquah. It has tosquats and squats. Its verf is another square pyramid.

Noncompact truncates

44. Tifipech - (TI fi pech) truncated fipech. It has topesquats and gads. Its verf is a pentagrammic pyramid.

45. Tifipped - (TI fip ed) truncated fipped. It has topepats and gads. Its verf is another a pentagrammic pyramid.

46. Tifiphexah - (TI fi fex ah) truncated fiphexah. It has tophexats and gads. Its verf is yet another pentagrammic pyramid.

Ditrigonary truncates

These are the truncates of three members of the ikhon regiment.

47. Tidditdih - (TID it dih) truncated ditdih. It has sidtids, tids, and tigids. Its verf is a ditrigon pyramid.

48. Tissidtipeppih - (tis id TI pep ih) truncated sidtipeppih. It has ditdids, tids, and topepats. Its verf is a tripod pyramid.

49. Tiggidtipeppih - (tig id TI pep ih) truncated gidtipeppih. It has gidtids, tigids, and topepats. Its verf is a tripod pyramid.

Freeloader truncates

These are the truncates of the freeloaders in category 1. All except tohah and tishahsah are orientable.

50. Tihipesh - (TI hi pesh) truncated ihipesh. It has tids, topeats, and thahs. Its verf is a bowtie pyramid.

51. Tidthah - (TID thah) truncated dithah. It has tuts, taztrats, and ditathas. Its verf is a tripod pyramid.

52. Totrihish - (TI tri hish) truncated trihish. It has toxats, toshexats, and thahs. Its verf is a bowtie pyramid.

53. Tidditchissah - (TID it chis ah) truncated ditchissah. It has tics, tasquats, and ditathas. Its verf is a tripod pyramid.

54. Tidditdohpah - (TID it do pa) truncated ditdohpah. It has tids, tazpats, and ditathas. Its verf is a tripod pyramid.

55. Tidthehihah - (TID theh hi ha) truncated dithehihah. It has toxats, tazhexats, and ditathas. Its verf is a tripod pyramid.

56. Tohah - (TO ha) truncated ohah. It has toes, taztrats, and shas. Its verf is a bowtie pyramid.

57. Tishahsah - (TI sha sa) truncated shahsah. It has tosquats, tasquats, and shas. Its verf is a bowtie pyramid.

Quasitruncates

These have strange pyramid verfs that cannot fit into euclidean space. All are orientable except quit trihish, quit ditchissah, and quit dithehihah.

58. Quit Pech - (QUIT pech) quasitruncated pechon. It has quiths and ikes.

59. Quothexah - (quo thex AH) quasitruncated hexah. It has quothats and tets.

60. Quit Shexah - (QUIT shex ah) quasitruncated shexah. It has quothats and octs.

61. Quothach - (QUO thach) quasitruncated hachon. It has quiths and trats.

62. Quit Phexah - (QUIT fex ah) quasitruncated phexah. It has quothats and ikes.

63. Quothihexah - (QUO thi hex ah) quasitruncated hihexah. It has quothats and trats.

64. Quitsquah - (QUIT squa) quasitruncated squah. It has quitsquats and cubes.

65. Quitsisquah - (QUIT sis qua) quasitruncated sisquah. It has quitsquats and squats.

66. Quit Fipech - (QUIT fip ech) quasitruncated fipech. It has quit pesquats and gads.

67. Quit Siddoh - (QUIT sid oh) quasitruncated siddoh. It has quit sissids and peats.

68. Quit Sipped - (QUIT sip ed) quasitruncated sipped. It has quit sissids and pepats.

69. Quit Fiphexah - (QUIT fi fex ah) quasitruncated fiphexah. It has quit phexats and gads.

70. Quit Shad - (QUIT shad) quasitruncated shad. It has quit sissids and phexats.

71. Quit Trihish - (QUIT tri hish) quasitruncated trihish. It has quothats, quit shexats, and thahs.

72. Quit Ditchissah - (QUIT dit chiss ah) quasitruncated ditchissah. It has quiths, quitasquats, and ditathas.

73. Quit Dithehihah - (QUIT dith e hi ha) quasitruncated dithehihah. It has quothats, quit azhexats, and ditathas.

74. Quit Shahsah - (QUIT sha sa) quasitruncated shahsah. It has quitsquats, quitasquats, and shas.

Alter-truncates

These were originally put into the prismatorhombate category despite not being prismatorhombates. Tidade, thacud, thazh, and tassish are all nonorientable. The rest are orientable.

Tapech regiment

142. Tapech - (TAP ech) truncated apech. It has ids, tis, and tuts.

143. Tidade - (tih DAYD) truncated dade. It has sidhids, tis, and tidetrats

144. Thacud - (THACK ud) truncated hacud. It has seihids, tuts, and tidetrats

Tahch regiment

145. Tahch - (TACH) truncated ahach. It has thats, hexats, and tuts.

146. Thazh - (THAZH) truncated hazh. It has hohas, hexats, and taztrats

147. Tippiph - (TIP if) truncated haca. It has thas, tuts, and taztrats

Tasquah regiment

148. Tasquah - (TASK wah) truncated asquah. It has coes, tosquats, and tics.

149. Tassish - (TASS ish) truncated sish. It has choes, tosquats, and thisquats

150. Tacish - (TACK ish) truncated cish. It has ohoes, tics, and thisquats

Tafpech regiment

151. Tafpech - (TAF pech) truncated afpech. It has dids, tigids, and topepats.

152. Tadoph - (TAD oaf) truncated doph. It has sidheis, tigids, and tophexat

153. Tippiph - (TIP if) truncated pepiph. It has gidheis, topepats, and tophexat
