List of hyperbolic honeycombs

This is a (forever) incomplete list of hyperbolic honeycombs. The style of this list and the names were inspired by Jonathan Bowers' list of uniform polychora, and Eric's list of uniform honeycombs.

Category 4: Square Rectates - These are the rectates of honeycombs with square edge figures, along with their regiments.
